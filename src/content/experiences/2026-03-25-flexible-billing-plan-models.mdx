I keep noticing a useful pattern in billing platforms: moving away from rigid plan groups and toward specific plan instances gives you more flexibility.

With instances, you can give one plan an exclusion without forcing every similar plan to inherit the same behavior. That gives the business more control and makes it easier to customize each plan on its own terms.

I know there are inheritance-style ways to do this, but sometimes the simpler model is the better one. If the product wants direct control over plan behavior, then the abstraction should stay close to that need.
