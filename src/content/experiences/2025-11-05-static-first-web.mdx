We locked the platform to static HTML + incremental hydration after perf traces showed 70% of visitors bounced before the hero finished animating. Shipping Astro with scoped islands kept the marketing team in MDX, gave us clean SEO, and reduced TTFB variance without strangling interactive widgets.
