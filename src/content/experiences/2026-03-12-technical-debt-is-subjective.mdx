I do not think technical debt is objective. The same architecture can look completely normal in one organization and like debt in another, depending on who is looking at it and what standards they are working from.

A shared PostgreSQL database with multiple services on top of it might be fine for one team. A new lead might look at the same setup and call it debt because their baseline is different.

That is why I do not try to chase a debt-free system as a universal goal. The label will keep changing with the organization, the time, and the expectations around it.
