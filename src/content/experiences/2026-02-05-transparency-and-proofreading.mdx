Something I value in a tech lead or architect is being transparent about the thought process. When I can show how I arrived at a decision, it is easier for people to trust that it is coming from the right state of mind and it opens the door to healthy debate.

What I am aiming for in a document for review is something that has already gone through my own first or second pass of correcting myself. GenAI can then help me catch blind spots I might have missed, but I still have to use my own judgment when something sounds off and reject it if it feels like a hallucination. Even after that, I know the outcome can still have gaps.
