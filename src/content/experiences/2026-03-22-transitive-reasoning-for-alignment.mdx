When I talk to product or engineering teams, I want to start with the facts that should stay true through the project. I think of those as non-functional facts: cost versus real time, quality versus speed, and other tradeoffs that should not drift every time the conversation changes.

That gives me a grounded way to prompt the design work. It is a lot like giving an AI a set of facts and asking it to reason from them instead of starting from a blank slate.

The moment I add conditions, I am also adding complexity. That is fine, but it should be explicit. If I accept the conditions, I am also accepting the extra work that comes with them.
