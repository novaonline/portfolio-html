I believe in evolution over revolution. I like gradual improvements more than radical changes.

I see the appeal of making a big move and resetting everything at once, but that is usually not the path I prefer. If the force behind a change is much bigger than what the team can absorb, it stops feeling like improvement and starts feeling like a reset.

I would rather keep the system moving forward in smaller steps. That feels more stable to me, and it gives the team more time to learn as the change happens instead of forcing everything to shift at once.
